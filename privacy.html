Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Philia Entreprise LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates as a technology and asset management holding company. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our institutional partners, clients, and visitors to our corporate website. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in connection with our institutional operations.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies strictly to the corporate holding entity, Philia Entreprise LLC, and its corporate website philiaentreprisellc.com. Owned technology products, operating subsidiaries, or real estate assets may maintain separate, dedicated privacy disclosures tailored to their specific consumer operations.

2. Information We Collect

As an institutional holding company, we limit data collection to the absolute minimum necessary for legal compliance and communication:

  • Inquiry Data: If you contact us directly via email, we collect your email address, name, organization, and any additional details you voluntarily share.
  • Technical Logging Data: Standard server logs are captured automatically by hosting providers to maintain security, analyze broad traffic patterns, and optimize site speed. This includes anonymized IP addresses, browser types, and access timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely for corporate operations:

  • To evaluate and respond to legal, regulatory, or business inquiries.
  • To secure our digital infrastructure against unauthorized access or cyber threats.
  • To comply with federal, state, and international regulatory requirements, including Apple Developer compliance.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Philia Entreprise LLC does not sell, lease, or share personal details with third-party advertisers or marketing firms. We may disclose collected information only under the following conditions:

  • With Subsidiaries and Affiliates: Information may be shared internally within our corporate group to address specific business opportunities or operational issues.
  • Legal and Regulatory Compliance: We will disclose information if required to do so by court order, applicable laws, or official requests from government authorities.

5. Security of Information

We use standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to defend your corporate information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized modification. Please note, however, that no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as entirely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain communications and technical server logs only for as long as necessary to address the purpose of collection, verify compliance, or fulfill statutory requirements.
